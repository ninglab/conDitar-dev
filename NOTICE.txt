Part I: conDitar
================================================================================

conDitar is a structure-based drug design model for pocket-conditioned 3D
molecule generation. It is built upon the TargetDiff codebase:

    TargetDiff: 3D Equivariant Diffusion for Target-Aware Molecule Generation
    and Affinity Prediction (ICLR 2023)
    Jiaqi Guan et al. | https://github.com/guanjq/targetdiff
    Licensed under the MIT License, Copyright (c) 2023 Jiaqi Guan

Portions of the code are also adapted from:

    Vector Neurons: A General Framework for SO(3)-Equivariant Networks
    Congyue Deng et al. | https://github.com/FlyingGiraffe/vnn
    Licensed under the MIT License, Copyright (c) 2021 Congyue Deng

    GVP-PyTorch | https://github.com/drorlab/gvp-pytorch
    Licensed under the MIT License, Copyright (c) 2020 Bowen Jing et al.

    liGAN | https://github.com/mattragoza/liGAN
